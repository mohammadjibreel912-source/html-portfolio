The Best Movies According to Mohammad Jibreel

My top 3 movies of all-time.

A Girl Walks Home Alone at Night

Perfect for fans of Drive. It's a gorgeous, black-and-white, highly stylized "Iranian vampire Western" with a cool indie soundtrack and heavy neo-noir vibes.

Paprika (Japan)

For fans of Spirited Away and Ex Machina. A breathtaking, psychological sci-fi anime about a machine that lets people enter dreams. It is a vibrant visual feast.

2046 (Hong Kong)

Directed by visual master Wong Kar-wai. It is a moody, neon-drenched romance that blends into a beautiful, artistic cyberpunk dreamscape.
